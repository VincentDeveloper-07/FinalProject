Welcome to the College of Science and Technology of The University of Cambodia. For everyone who wishes to pursue any AA and BA degree of the study programs, please register now or visit our campus of The University of Cambodia.

Our Information Technology Management for Business (ITMB) degree covers a vibrant mix of transferable skills and knowledge for those who want exciting and challenging management or professional careers designing, developing, and implementing technology solutions for business. The ITMB degree is designed to offer insight into the real world of IT. It allows you to learn the people, project, and management skills required to be a successful IT professional.

Program Learning Outcomes

1 - As a graduate, you will be prepared to reliably demonstrate the ability to:
2 - Analyze business problems and make ethically sound strategic decisions enabled by Information Technology (IT).
3 - Manage a business technology project within a cross-functional and multi-disciplinary team by applying project management principles.
4 - Adopt operations management principles and information technology to improve the efficiency and effectiveness of business processes.
5 - Propose strategic and operational solutions by applying principles of management accounting and financial analysis.
6 - Design information technology solutions that automate and support business processes.
7 - Model ethical, legal, and professional codes of conduct of the Canadian business environment and global marketplace.
8 - Build data solutions to support business operations, strategic decision-making, and business planning to improve organizational performance and ensure adequate risk management.
9 - Evaluate organizational structure and management processes, with a focus on small and medium-sized enterprises, to improve organizational performance.
10 - Provide direction about the opportunities for the integration of technology, commerce, and marketing to support innovative digital business models.
